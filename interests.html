Research Interests


Artificial Intelligence is reshaping how we interact with technology, from improving business operations to creating smarter, more personalized experiences in our daily lives. Whether it’s analyzing text data or detecting patterns in complex datasets, AI is a powerful tool that continues to unlock new possibilities.

My work centers around using machine learning and NLP to build systems that not only solve problems but also make data more meaningful and accessible. For more details, please see my Experience and Projects.

Here are a few areas I’m particularly passionate about:


  • Using AI to Understand and Analyze Language: I’m fascinated by how natural language processing (NLP) can help machines understand and interact with human language. From chatbots to text analysis, I enjoy exploring how we can apply these tools to make communication with technology smoother and more intuitive.
  • Improving Data Quality Through Smart Detection: Data is the foundation of any AI system, but it’s not always perfect. I’m interested in how we can use AI to find and fix errors in data, making sure that the insights we draw are accurate and reliable.
  • Making AI Models More Transparent and Trustworthy: As AI becomes more powerful, it’s important to ensure that we understand how these models make decisions. I’m dedicated to exploring ways to make AI systems more explainable and secure, so they can be trusted to make decisions in critical areas like healthcare or finance.
