Masters in Applied Computer Science

Concordia University, Montreal, Canada.

Pursuing a Masters in Applied Computer Science at Concordia University was a transformative journey that significantly advanced my career and skills, building on my past hands-on experience in the field. While my career provided practical insights into machine learning and AI, the degree allowed me to delve deeper into the mathematical and statistical foundations behind these technologies. I explored advanced topics such as machine learning, computer vision, algorithm design, and distributed systems, gaining a robust foundation for tackling complex problems. Through hands-on projects and research, I developed and fine-tuned deep learning models, optimized large-scale systems, and experimented with latest concepts like Federated Learning and Conversational AI. The rigorous academic environment sharpened my analytical abilities and technical expertise, while collaborative projects and peer interactions enhanced my teamwork and communication skills. Finally, I extend my heartfelt gratitude to everyone who supported and believed in me throughout this invaluable journey.

Pursuing a Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science & Engineering at the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies, Dehradun provided me with a solid foundation in both theoretical and applied aspects of computing.
